Campus Life

The schoolday starts at 8:25. There are 8 periods, a lunch, and an extra period. Each period is 45 minutes long. ILA is 2 periods. There is an A lunch for 6th graders, a B lunch for 7th graders, and a C lunch for 8th graders.


Campus Life

school library

Was established in 1978

The school library is where the kids read. The students go to the library once every 4 weeks. You can check out up to four books in that period.

school gym

Was built in 1980

The gym is where athletes practice sports, and physical education.

school cafeteria

Was built in 1982

The school cafeteria is where students eat food. They can either bring their lunch from home, or buy their lunch in the cafeteria. There are 3 different lunch schedules. There is A Lunch, B Lunch, and C Lunch. A Lunch is for 6th graders, B Lunch is for 7th graders, and C Lunch is for 8th graders.
